# 🔮 Future Roadmap - What's Next for Next-Videos

We are constantly evolving to provide the best video downloading experience. Below is our vision for the next generation of Next-Videos applications.

## 📱 Mobile Applications
We are planning to bring Next-Videos to your pocket with native mobile apps that allow for one-tap downloads and built-in media players.
- **Android App:** Native APK for fast performance and background downloading support.
- **iOS App:** A secure, mobile-friendly interface for managing your media on the go.

## 💻 Desktop Applications
Experience Next-Videos with the power of a standalone desktop application.
- **Windows App:** A dedicated `.exe` with deeper system integration and faster processing.
- **Linux App:** Native `.deb` and `.rpm` packages for the open-source community.
- **macOS App:** A sleek, optimized version for Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

## 🔗 Browser Extensions
Quick-download buttons right inside your browser.
- **Chrome / Edge Extension:** One-click download button added directly to YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram pages.
- **Firefox Add-on:** Privacy-focused extension for seamless media extraction.

## 🚀 Upcoming Core Features
- **Batch Downloading:** Download entire playlists or multiple URLs at once.
- **Cloud Storage Integration:** Automatically sync your downloads to Google Drive or Dropbox.
- **Advanced Format Conversion:** Convert videos to high-quality GIFs or specific audio bitrates on the fly.
